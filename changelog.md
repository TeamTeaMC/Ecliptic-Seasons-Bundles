**Changes**

* Added compatibility for **Quark snowy blocks**. Snow can now render on hollow logs, vertical slabs, posts, and similar blocks.
* Updated **Regions Unexplored (RU)** integration:

    * Added seasonal texture support for the **flowering lily pad**.
    * Updated related resource pack entries.
* Added **No Man's Land tags** to prevent certain blocks from retaining snow.
* Removed **Magnolia Leaves** from the `none_fallen_leaves` tag.
